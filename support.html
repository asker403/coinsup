About the App

Coin Flip is a modern, multilingual coin flipping application that offers an engaging way to make decisions or play games. The app features realistic coin flip animations with 5 different currencies and supports 5 languages.

Features

  • Realistic 3D coin flip animations
  • Multiple currency support (TL, USD, EUR, RUB, CNY)
  • 5 language support (English, Turkish, French, Russian, Chinese)
  • Statistics tracking
  • Detailed flip history

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the language?

Tap the language button in the top-left corner of the screen to cycle through available languages.

How do I change the currency?

Use the left and right arrows next to the currency name, or long press on the currency name to cycle through available currencies.

How do I view my statistics?

Tap the chart icon in the top-right corner to view your flip statistics and history.
